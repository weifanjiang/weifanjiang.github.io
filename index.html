I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at Harvard University. My research interest is broadly in computer systems, including computer networks and security. I am also passionate about applying machine learning to solve system-related problems.
Prior to Harvard, I received MS in Computer Science from Columbia University in May 2021, and BS with double majors in Computer Science and ACMS (Applied and Computational Math Sciences, Discrete Math and Algorithms option) from University of Washington, Seattle in June 2019.
